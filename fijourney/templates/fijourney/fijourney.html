— Early Retirement: situation of quitting your job/career and pursuing other activities that you’re passionate about.

FAQ's

What is FIRE?
Financial Independence and Early Retirement (FIRE) is a lifestyle movement that aims at reducing expenditures and increasing investments in order to achieve financial freedom and retirement faster at an early age.

This means freedom to pursue activities you want without worrying much about money. Financial independence also often invites Early Retirement/ Retiring Early (RE), i.e. not having to work at a job just for the sake of money. Although FI/RE is a relatively new concept, however, there are multiple examples of individuals who have achieved financial independence in their 40s, 30s and sometimes even in their 20s.
Who is FIRE for?
FIRE lifestyle is for all those who want to achieve financial independence quickly and ready to optimize their expenses and invest smartly.
